About ADBSA Aeta Integrated School

ADBSA Aeta Integrated School is a learning institution committed to providing quality, inclusive, and culturally-responsive education for Indigenous learners in Sitio Banag, Palanginan, Iba, Zambales.

Through strong partnerships with stakeholders, the Department of Education, and the local community, the school continuously nurtures learners to become responsible, resilient, and lifelong learners while preserving the rich heritage and traditions of the Ayta people.

  • ✔ Inclusive Education
  • ✔ Child-Friendly School
  • ✔ Indigenous Peoples Education (IPEd)
  • ✔ Community Partnership

Our Vision, Mission & Core Values

"Pangarap Nyo ay Sama-sama Nating Aabutin."

At ADBSA Aeta Integrated School, we believe that every child possesses unique talents and limitless potential. Together with our dedicated teachers, parents, Indigenous Peoples community, local government, and education partners, we nurture dreams, build character, strengthen knowledge, and prepare every learner to become a responsible citizen and future leader.

Guided by the Department of Education's commitment to provide quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education for every learner.

👁️

Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to nation-building.

🎯

Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:

Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment.

Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.

Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.

Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.
